Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to publicize our services through the Internet. As a decoration company we want to show at a glance what kind of events we cover, as well as the packages and promotions; this will generate more benefits for the growth of our company.

Audience

Parents, grooms and brides, companies, moms-to-be will be our main target. People who want to celebrate their birthdays, a graduation, a baby shower, a baby gender reveal, a wedding and more events, they will not need to worry about decoration,they can trust in our hands and that everything will be on time as they expect.

Content

Home page

Do you want to have an unforgettable event? We are here to help you, we have the best plans for your event. You won't have to worry about the decoration, we will take care of everything, we will make your event a success. We count with a variety of plans for you to choose from, you can choose the one that fits your needs.

Images for the Home page


Events

Looking for something specific? We have a variety of events that you can choose from, let us show you how we can make your event a success. Here is a list of some of our events: sex revelation, baby shower, birthday party, graduation party, Christmas party, weddings, theme parties, and corporate events.

We have three modalities, you can choose the one that suits your needs: delivery of decorations, which consists of working on the decorations for the event, and set a delivery date to take it to a previously agreed place or the client can pick it up at our facilities; make and assemble decoration, the team will move to the place of the event designated by the client to decorate it; and complete package, we have events hall which will be rented and all the decoration will be assembled there.
