Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to expand our market through the internet. As a company, we want to reach more customers and tell them what we can do for them, our safety plans, and share information about our promotions; this will generate more profits for our company and let us grow.

Audience

Workers, parents, and friends will be our target audience. People who want a safe place where spend a day, where they can relax and disconnect from daily tasks and duties, and people who want to have an unforgettable experience and enjoy nature. Our typography, colors, and content will let them connect with us at a glance, by choosing color theory.
